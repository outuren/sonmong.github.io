How it started?

Everything dated back to when I was in my home country. I would spend hours to draw using only pencil and paper.

I tried to enhance my skills through digital drawing. However at that time, drawing and designing is no more than just a hobby.

But to a point of my life, I realized that I have to focus and dedicate my time and effort if I want to pursuit a professional career. That is the reason why I attend Miami University.
